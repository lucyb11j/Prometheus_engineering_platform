# 1. Executive Summary

The Product Roadmap defines the strategic evolution of the Prometheus Analytics Platform.

The roadmap aligns business objectives, technical architecture, analytics capabilities, and machine learning initiatives into a structured implementation plan.

The roadmap is designed to progressively deliver value while reducing implementation risk.

---

# 2. Product Vision

## Vision Statement

Create a unified analytics ecosystem that enables infrastructure and industrial organizations to monitor operations in real time, predict future outcomes, optimize resources, and support executive decision-making.
